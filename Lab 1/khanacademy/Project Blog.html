Exploring Almaty’s Nature

06.25.2019

Almaty is a paradise for nature enthusiasts! Whether you're hiking the trails of Ile-Alatau National Park, enjoying the serene beauty of Big Almaty Lake, or taking in the breathtaking views from Kok Tobe, this city never ceases to amaze. The mountains provide the perfect escape from the urban hustle, offering fresh air, stunning landscapes, and unforgettable adventures. Plan your visit, and don’t forget your camera! #AlmatyAdventures #NatureLovers

Must-Try Kazakh Dishes

06.27.2019

Visiting Kazakhstan? You can’t leave without trying its incredible cuisine! Start with beshbarmak, the country’s signature dish, followed by juicy manty and flavorful laghman. Don’t miss the chance to taste kazy—a unique horse meat sausage—and finish your meal with sweet chak-chak. Kazakh food is all about comfort, rich flavors, and tradition. What’s your favorite Kazakh dish? #KazakhCuisine #FoodieAdventures.

Almaty’s Hidden Gems

06.28.2019

Beyond the famous attractions, Almaty has countless hidden gems waiting to be discovered. Stroll through the charming streets of Arbat for local art and souvenirs, visit the peaceful Botanical Garden for a quiet escape, or explore the Green Bazaar to experience the city’s vibrant daily life. Each corner of Almaty has a story to tell—are you ready to uncover it?
