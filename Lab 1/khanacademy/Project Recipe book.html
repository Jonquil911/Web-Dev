Simple Fried Eggs Recipee

Contents:

  1. Ingredients
  2. Instructions

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the butter or oil to the pan and let it melt or heat up evenly.
  3. Crack the eggs gently into the pan, ensuring the yolks remain intact.
  4. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and black pepper over the eggs.
  5. Cook the eggs until the whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your preference:
    • For sunny side up, do not flip the eggs.
    • For over easy, carefully flip the eggs and cook for another 30 seconds.
    • For over hard, cook until the yolks are firm.
  6. Remove the eggs from the pan using a spatula and serve immediately.

Enjoy your fried eggs with toast, salad, or as part of a hearty breakfast!
